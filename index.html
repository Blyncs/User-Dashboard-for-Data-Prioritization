🛣️ HIDOT & Blyncsy Maintenance Prioritization Dashboard

Input roadway parameters and upload a Blyncsy GeoJSON to generate a ranked asset priority matrix and interactive map.

ℹ️ Priority scores are a composite of three factors (0–100): 50% Traffic Severity (VMT, speed, AADT exposure) + 30% Cost-Effectiveness (safety impact per dollar — cheap quick-wins rank up) + 20% Timeframe Urgency (⚡ quick fixes boosted; 🏗 capital projects reduced). PASER and Retroreflectivity are extracted from loaded files. Upload one or more GeoJSON files — or connect a WFS endpoint — to activate the map.
